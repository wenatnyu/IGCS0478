投影技术分类 / Projector Technology Classification

现代投影设备技术原理 / Modern Projection Device Technology Principles

LCD投影技术

  • 利用液晶面板控制光线通过
  • 三片式设计提供高分辨率
  • 色彩还原度好、亮度均匀

LCD Projection Technology

  • Uses liquid crystal panels to control light transmission
  • Three-panel design provides high resolution
  • Excellent color reproduction and uniform brightness

DLP投影技术

  • DMD芯片控制微镜片反射
  • 高对比度和清晰的黑白效果
  • 快速响应和长使用寿命

DLP Projection Technology

  • DMD chips control micromirror reflection
  • High contrast ratio and crisp black-white effects
  • Fast response and long service life

LED投影技术

  • 无灯泡设计，使用寿命长
  • 绿色节能、维护成本低
  • 纯色彩输出和快速开机

LED Projection Technology

  • Lamp-free design with long service life
  • Green energy efficiency, low maintenance costs
  • Pure color output and rapid startup
  • 亮度和对比度 / Brightness and contrast ratio
  • 色彩表现与准确性 / Color performance and accuracy
  • 使用成本和维护 / Usage costs and maintenance
  • 使用寿命和可靠性 / Service life and reliability
