LCD投影原理 / LCD Projection Principles

三片式液晶投影技术 / Three-Panel Liquid Crystal Projection Technology

光路系统
  • 金属卤化物灯或LED光源
  • 光线通过偏振器和分光镜
  • RGB三色光线分离处理
液晶面板控制
  • 三块液晶面板对应RGB三色
  • 电压控制液晶分子旋转
  • 通过性变化实现灰度控制
成像原理
  • 三色光线经过液晶面板调制
  • 合光镜将RGB光线合成
  • 投影镜头放大成像在屏幕上
Optical Path System
  • Metal halide lamp or LED light source
  • Light passes through polarizer and dichroic mirror
  • RGB three-color light separation processing
Liquid Crystal Panel Control
  • Three LCD panels correspond to RGB colors
  • Voltage controls liquid crystal molecule rotation
  • Transparency variation achieves grayscale control
Image Formation Principle
  • Three-color light modulated by LCD panels
  • Combiner mirror synthesizes RGB light
  • Projection lens magnifies image onto screen
