存储设备类型 | Storage Device Types

SSD endurance

SSD耐久性

The total number of times data can be guaranteed to be written to or read from a solid-state drive (SSD) over its useful life.

固态驱动器（SSD）在其可用生命周期内可以保证写入或读取数据的总次数。

optical storage

光学存储

A type of storage that uses a laser to read and write data; this includes CDs, DVDs and Blu-ray discs.

使用激光读写数据的存储类型，包括CD、DVD和蓝光光盘。

dual layering

双层

The use of two recording layers in storage media such as DVDs and some Blu-ray.

在DVD和某些蓝光等存储媒体中使用两个记录层。
