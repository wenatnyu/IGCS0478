时钟与执行 | Clock & Execution

system clock

系统时钟

Generates timing signals on the control bus to ensure synchronisation.

在控制总线上产生定时信号以确保同步。

clock cycle

时钟周期

The clock speed is measured in GHz; this is the oscillation frequency of the system clock which sends out pulses along the control bus; for example, a 3.5 GHz clock cycle means 3.5 billion clock cycles per second.

时钟速度以GHz为单位测量；这是系统时钟的振动频率，它沿控制总线发送脉冲；例如，3.5 GHz时钟周期意味着每秒35亿个时钟周期。
