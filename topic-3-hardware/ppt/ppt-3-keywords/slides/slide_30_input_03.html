指向设备与触摸屏 | Pointing Devices & Touch Screens

optical mouse

光学鼠标

A pointing device that uses a red LED to track the movement of the device and then pass its coordinates to the computer.

使用红色LED跟踪设备移动然后将其坐标传递给计算机的指向设备。

touch screen

触摸屏

A screen that allows the user to select or manipulate screen images by touching with a finger or stylus; touch screens most commonly use capacitive, infra-red or resistive technology.

允许用户使用手指或触控笔触摸选择或操作屏幕图像的屏幕；触摸屏最常使用电容式、红外或电阻式技术。
