cloud storage

云存储

A method of data storage where data is stored on off-site servers; the physical storage may be spread over hundreds of servers in many locations

数据存储在场外服务器上的存储方法；物理存储可能分布在许多位置的数百台服务器上

data redundancy

数据冗余

The unnecessary storage of the same data simultaneously on more than one storage device

同时在多个存储设备上不必要地存储相同数据
