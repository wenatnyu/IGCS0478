取指-译码-执行周期详解

Step-by-Step Breakdown of the Instruction Cycle

1. 取指 (Fetch)

PC中的地址复制到MAR

Address from PC copied to MAR

指令从内存检索到MDR

Instruction retrieved from memory to MDR

指令复制到CIR

Instruction copied to CIR

PC递增指向下一条指令

PC incremented to next instruction

2. 译码 (Decode)

CU检查指令的二进制模式

CU examines instruction's binary pattern

确定操作码和操作数

Determines opcode and operand

向其他组件发送准备信号

Sends preparation signals to other components

3. 执行 (Execute)

执行已译码的指令

Performs the decoded instruction

可能涉及ALU计算、数据移动或内存操作

May involve ALU calculations, data movement, or memory operations

完成后重复周期

Cycle repeats upon completion
