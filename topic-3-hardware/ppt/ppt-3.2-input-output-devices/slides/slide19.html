打印机技术原理 / Printer Technology Principles

激光打印机内部结构与工作流程 / Laser Printer Internal Structure and Workflow

激光成像单元
  • 激光器产生精确光束
  • 扫描镜系统控制光束位置
  • 在感光鼓上形成静电像
印刷过程
  • 感光鼓带电吸附墨粉
  • 转印辊将墨粉转印到纸张
  • 定影器加热固化墨粉
墨粉系统
  • 墨粉仓存储和供给
  • 显影辊控制墨粉分布
  • 清洁单元处理废料
Laser Imaging Unit
  • Laser generates precise light beam
  • Scanning mirror system controls beam position
  • Forms electrostatic image on photosensitive drum
Printing Process
  • Photosensitive drum electrically attracts toner
  • Transfer roller moves toner to paper
  • Fuser heats and fixes toner permanently
Toner System
  • Toner cartridge stores and supplies toner
  • Developer roller controls toner distribution
  • Cleaning unit processes waste materials
① 充电 / Charging: 感光鼓表面带负电
② 曝光 / Exposure: 激光扫描消除电荷
③ 显影 / Development: 墨粉吸附到有电区域
④ 转印 / Transfer: 墨粉转印到纸张
⑤ 定影 / Fusing: 高温固化墨粉
⑥ 清洁 / Cleaning: 清洁感光鼓
