complementary metal oxide semi-conductor (CMOS)

互补金属氧化物半导体（CMOS）

A chip that produces a current (or pulse) when light falls onto its surface

当光线照射到其表面时产生电流（或脉冲）的芯片

digital signal processor (DSP)

数字信号处理器（DSP）

A processor that calculates, for example, the coordinates for a pointing device based on the electrical pulses received

例如，基于接收到的电脉冲计算指向设备坐标的处理器

optical character recognition

光学字符识别

Technology that can convert hard copy text or images into a digital format for storage in computer memory

可以将硬拷贝文本或图像转换为数字格式以存储在计算机内存中的技术
