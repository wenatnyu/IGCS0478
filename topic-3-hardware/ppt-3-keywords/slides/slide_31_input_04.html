触摸屏技术 | Touch Screen Technology

电容式触摸屏

capacitive touch screen

当被手指或触控笔触摸时利用屏幕电容（存储电荷的能力）变化的触摸屏类型。

A type of touch screen that makes use of the change in capacitance (ability to store charge) of the screen when touched by a finger or stylus.

红外触摸屏

infra-red touch screen

使用红外光束和传感器检测屏幕触摸位置的触摸屏类型。

A type of touch screen that uses infra-red light beams and sensors to detect where the screen has been touched.

电阻式触摸屏

resistive touch screen

使用两个导电层在屏幕被触摸的地方接触的触摸屏类型。

A type of touch screen that uses two conductive layers that touch where the screen has been touched.
