恶意软件类型

Types of Malware
Spyware
间谍软件

Malware that gathers information by monitoring user activities and sends it to cybercriminals.

通过监控用户活动收集信息并将其发送给网络犯罪分子的恶意软件。

Adware
广告软件

Malware that attempts to flood users with unwanted advertising.

试图用不需要的广告淹没最终用户的恶意软件类型。

Ransomware
勒索软件

Malware that encrypts user data and "holds it hostage" until ransom is paid.

加密用户计算机上的数据并"劫持数据"直到支付赎金的恶意软件。
