网络钓鱼与欺诈

Phishing & Fraud
Phishing
网络钓鱼

Sending legitimate-looking emails designed to trick recipients into revealing personal details.

发送看似合法的电子邮件，旨在欺骗收件人提供其个人详细信息。

Spear Phishing
鱼叉式网络钓鱼

Targeted phishing attacking specific people or organisations rather than blanket attacks.

针对特定人员或组织而不是进行全面攻击的定向网络钓鱼。

Pharming
域名劫持

Redirecting users to fake websites to illegally obtain personal data without their knowledge.

将用户重定向到虚假网站以在用户不知情的情况下非法获取个人数据。

DNS Cache Poisoning
DNS缓存投毒

Altering IP addresses on DNS to redirect browsers to fake websites.

更改DNS上的IP地址，旨在将用户浏览器重定向到虚假网站。
