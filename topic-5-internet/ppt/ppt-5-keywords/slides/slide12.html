网络安全威胁

Cyber Security Threats
Wardriving
战争驾驶

Using laptop, antenna, GPS and software to intercept Wi-Fi signals illegally.

使用笔记本电脑、天线、GPS设备和软件拦截Wi-Fi信号并非法获取数据。

WEP Encryption
有线等效隐私加密协议

An algorithm for wireless networks to protect against data interception.

用于保护无线网络免受数据拦截的算法。

DoS Attack
拒绝服务攻击

A cyberattack that disrupts normal website operation by flooding it with requests.

通过向网站发送大量请求来破坏网站正常运行的网络攻击。

DDoS Attack
分布式拒绝服务攻击

A DoS attack where fake requests come from many different computers, making it harder to stop.

虚假请求来自许多不同计算机的DoS攻击，更难阻止。
