数字货币与区块链

Digital Currency & Blockchain
Timestamp
时间戳

A digital record of the date and time that a data block is created in blockchain networks.

区块链网络中创建数据块的日期和时间的数字记录。

Proof-of-Work
工作量证明

The algorithm used in blockchain to confirm transactions and produce new blocks; miners complete and monitor transactions for rewards.

区块链网络中用于确认交易并生成新区块的算法；矿工完成并监控交易以获得奖励。
