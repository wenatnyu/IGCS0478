对称加密应用 (Symmetric Applications)

  • 文件加密File encryption for local files and databases
  • VPN隧道VPN tunnel data transmission
  • 流媒体Real-time audio/video encryption

非对称加密应用 (Asymmetric Applications)

  • HTTPSSecure website connections
  • 电子邮件Email encryption and digital signatures
  • 数字证书SSL/TLS certificate authentication
  • 区块链Cryptocurrency transaction verification

混合加密方案 (Hybrid Encryption)

在实际应用中，通常结合使用两种加密方式。非对称加密用于安全地交换对称密钥，然后使用对称加密来高效地传输大量数据。
In practice, both methods are used together. Asymmetric encryption for secure key exchange, and symmetric encryption for efficient data transmission.
