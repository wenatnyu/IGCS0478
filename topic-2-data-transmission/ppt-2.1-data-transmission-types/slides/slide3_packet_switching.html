分组交换

Packet Switching

数据被分解成多个数据包，独立发送并在接收端重新组装的传输方法。

A method where data is broken into multiple packets, sent independently, and reassembled at the receiver.

分组交换过程详解 (Process of Packet Switching)

1

数据被分解成数据包

Data is broken down into packets

2

每个数据包可以走不同路径

Each packet could take a different route

3

路由器控制数据包路径

A router controls the route a packet takes

4

数据包可能乱序到达

Packets may arrive out of order

5

最后一个数据包到达后，数据包被重新排序

Once the last packet has arrived, packets are reordered

优点 (Advantages)

  • 无需建立单一通信线路 No need to establish a single communication line
  • 可克服失败或繁忙节点 Can overcome failed or busy nodes
  • 高数据传输速度 High data transmission speed
  • 易于扩展 Easy to scale

缺点 (Disadvantages)

  • 数据包可能丢失 Packets may be lost
  • 实时流媒体更容易出错 Real-time streaming more prone to errors
  • 接收端重新排序时的延迟 Delay during reordering at receiver
