十进制转二进制 / Denary to Binary Conversion

方法说明 / Method Description

除以2取余法 / Division by 2 Method

连续除以2，记录余数，然后从下往上读取余数。
Repeatedly divide by 2, record the remainders, then read them from bottom to top.

步骤说明 / Step-by-Step Process

  • 步骤 1 / Step 1: 将十进制数除以2
    Divide the denary number by 2
  • 步骤 2 / Step 2: 记录商和余数
    Record quotient and remainder
  • 步骤 3 / Step 3: 对商继续除以2，直到商为0
    Continue dividing quotient by 2 until quotient is 0
  • 步骤 4 / Step 4: 从底部向上读取余数
    Read remainders from bottom to top

例子 / Example: 转换 214₁₀ → ?₂

从下往上读取余数 / Reading Remainders Bottom to Top ↑ 11010110
最终结果 / Final Result: 214₁₀ = 11010110₂

重要说明 / Important Notes

  • 当数字不能被2整除时，余数为1 / When a number is odd, the remainder is 1.
  • 当数字能被2整除时，余数为0 / When a number is even, the remainder is 0.
  • 必须继续除法直到商为0 / Must continue until the quotient becomes 0.
  • 最后的余数是最高位 / The last remainder is the Most Significant Bit (MSB).
